Sell or Rent Your Content

Blockchain technology enables true ownership: you own your content, so you should be able to sell or re-sell content. This dynamic isnâ€™t really possible with web 2.0: your content may include your name but it is typically not cryptographically signed nor stored on a public ledger. Ownership in web 2.0 is less clear and requires legal contracts for enforcement.
